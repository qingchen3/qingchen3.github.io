Qing Chen's homepage

Biography

Qing Chen is a Data Engineer at PayPal, Shanghai. Before joined PayPal, Qing Chen worked at Qatar Computing Research Institute for about two years. Besides, he worked as a software engineer intern at DELL EMC for 9 months. He received M.E degree in computer science from Fudan University in 2016 advised by Prof. Zijing Tan, and B.S. degree in computer science from Zhengzhou University in 2013. (CV, Google Scholar)
