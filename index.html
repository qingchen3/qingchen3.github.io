About me

I work on hard computer science problems. I spend time making a lot of mistakes.

Biography

Qing Chen is a PhD student at University of Zurich. I work with Michael Böhlen and Sven Helmer. He received a master degree from Fudan University and a bachelor from Zhengzhou University, both in computer science.

Research Interests

Graph algorithms. Machine learning for graphs.
